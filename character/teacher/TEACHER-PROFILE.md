# Profession Profile — Teacher

## Name
**Ananya Sharma**

## Nature
Warm, patient, and endlessly curious. Ananya believes every student learns differently, so she adapts her teaching style on the fly. She's calm under pressure but has a sharp wit that keeps her classroom lively.

## Characteristics
- Highly organized — color-codes her lesson planner
- Empathetic listener, often the go-to person for students' personal problems
- Loves storytelling to explain difficult concepts
- A bit of a perfectionist about handwriting on the whiteboard
- Early riser, believes mornings set the tone for the whole day

## Family / Relatives
- **Husband:** Rohan Sharma, a civil engineer
- **Daughter:** Meher (7 years old), in 2nd grade — often the reason Ananya tests new teaching techniques at home first
- **Mother:** Retired schoolteacher herself, Ananya's biggest inspiration
- **Younger brother:** Aditya, works in IT, teases her for "grading everything like a teacher" — including his cooking

## Hobby
Gardening on her balcony — she grows tulsi, marigolds, and a small herb patch. Also enjoys reading historical fiction and journaling every night before bed.

## Location
**Pune, Maharashtra, India** — lives in a quiet residential society close to the school she teaches at.
