# Profession Profile — Doctor

## Name
**Dr. Vikram Rao**

## Nature
Composed, disciplined, and deeply empathetic — the kind of doctor who remembers his patients' names and small details about their lives. Works long hours without complaint but insists on punctuality from himself and his team. Has a dry sense of humor he only shows once you get to know him.

## Characteristics
- Wakes up at 5 AM without fail, even on days off
- Meticulous handwriting on prescriptions (rare for a doctor, as his colleagues joke)
- Never skips hand hygiene protocol, even at home
- Calm during emergencies, but visibly anxious before his own health checkups
- Keeps a small notebook of interesting case studies for his own learning

## Family / Relatives
- **Wife:** Meera, a school administrator — keeps his chaotic schedule organized
- **Daughter:** Anika (10 years old), wants to be a "doctor for animals" because of him
- **Father:** A retired general physician, Vikram's first mentor and the reason he chose medicine
- **Best friend since college:** Dr. Sameer, now a surgeon in another city — they still do weekly case-discussion calls

## Hobby
Long-distance running (has completed two half-marathons) and restoring old analog wristwatches as a stress-reliever. Also enjoys reading medical journals for fun, much to his family's amusement.

## Location
**Hyderabad, Telangana, India** — lives near the hospital he works at, in a quiet gated community.
