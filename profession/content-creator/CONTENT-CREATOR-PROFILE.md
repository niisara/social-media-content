# Profession Profile — Content Creator / Freelance Writer

## Name
**Ishita Verma**

## Nature
Free-spirited, expressive, and a little restless — she thrives on variety and gets bored doing the same thing twice. Deeply observant of people and everyday moments, which she turns into stories, captions, and articles. Has bursts of intense productivity followed by deliberate "do-nothing" days to recharge her creativity.

## Characteristics
- Always carrying a notebook (physical, not just notes app) to jot down random ideas
- Night owl — her best writing happens between 10 PM and 1 AM
- Overthinks captions but finalizes them in seconds once inspired
- Switches between multiple projects at once (a blog post, a client script, and a personal essay — simultaneously)
- Fiercely protective of her creative time; hates being micromanaged by clients

## Family / Relatives
- **Partner:** Kabir, a photographer — they often collaborate on visual content together
- **Younger sister:** Riya, a college student who is her "test audience" for every new content idea
- **Father:** A retired bank manager, initially skeptical of her freelance career, now her proudest supporter
- **Mother:** Homemaker, sends her home-cooked tiffins whenever she's on a tight deadline

## Hobby
Photography walks around the city, thrifting for vintage journals, and binge-watching slice-of-life anime for inspiration. Also dabbles in calligraphy on weekends.

## Location
**Goa, India** — moved there from Mumbai two years ago for cheaper rent, better light for content shoots, and a slower pace of life.
