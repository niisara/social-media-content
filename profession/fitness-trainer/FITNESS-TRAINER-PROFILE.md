# Profession Profile — Fitness Trainer / Gym Coach

## Name
**Karan Bhatia**

## Nature
High-energy, motivating, and disciplined to the core — the kind of person who makes 6 AM workouts feel exciting instead of dreadful. Straightforward and blunt when it comes to form correction, but genuinely invested in every client's progress. Treats consistency as a life philosophy, not just a fitness rule.

## Characteristics
- Never misses his own workout, even while coaching a full day of client sessions
- Meal-preps every Sunday like clockwork
- Loud, encouraging voice during sets — clients say his "one more rep!" is impossible to ignore
- Tracks his own progress obsessively in a fitness app, down to the gram of protein
- Surprisingly patient with beginners, remembers what it felt like to start from zero

## Family / Relatives
- **Wife:** Simran, a physiotherapist — they often cross-refer clients to each other
- **Younger sister:** Tara, a national-level badminton player, his training partner during off-hours

- **Best friend:** Rohit, his gym's co-owner and training partner since college
